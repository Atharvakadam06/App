# StuGrow - Student Network

A professional social platform designed exclusively for college students.

## How to Open

**Just double-click `dist/index.html`** - the app will open directly in your browser!

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up** - Create your account with your college email
2. **Complete your profile** - Add your college, branch, and bio
3. **Start connecting** - Share posts, upload papers, list books, and connect with students

## Features

- **Feed** - Share updates, achievements with campus
- **Network** - Connect with students (called "Link")
- **PYQ Vault** - Upload and share previous year question papers
- **Book Exchange** - List and request free textbooks
- **Mentor Hub** - Share tips and advice for freshers
- **Messages** - Direct messaging with other students
- **Profile** - Edit your profile and view your activity
- **Settings** - Dark/Light mode toggle

## All Data is Real

- No fake users, posts, or content
- All data is created by real users
- Posts, papers, books, and tips persist in your browser
- Sign up to create your account and start contributing
